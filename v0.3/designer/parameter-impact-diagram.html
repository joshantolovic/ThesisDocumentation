Control Panel: Parameter Impact Map

What each inspector slider actually does

üå± Generation Parameters

plantCount 90

How many plants spawn at the start of the simulation.

Impact:
  • Higher = more competition
  • Higher = more crowding (etiolation)
  • Higher = faster moisture depletion
seed 0

Random seed for plant placement and vigor values.

Impact:
  • Different seeds = different starting layouts
  • Same seed = reproducible results
  • Use "New Seed" button for variety
defaultPlantLength 0.02

Starting height of all plants at birth.

Impact:
  • Visual only - doesn't affect gameplay
  • Higher = plants start taller
defaultPlantWidth 0.14

Starting thickness/root spread of all plants.

Impact:
  • Higher = larger starting root radius
  • Higher = easier early water access
defaultRootSpreadDistance 1.0

Base root radius before width growth bonuses.

Impact:
  • Higher = plants can reach further water
  • Higher = more neighbor detection
  • Critical survival parameter
‚ö†Ô∏è
Too low: Plants can't reach water tiles, instant death.
Too high: All plants overlap, extreme crowding.

üìà Growth Parameters

checkInterval 1.0s

How often (in seconds) plants check conditions and grow.

Impact:
  • Lower = faster growth cycles
  • Lower = more CPU usage
  • Affected by Time Scale multiplier
growthIncrementLength 0.002

Base amount plants grow taller per cycle (before modifiers).

Impact:
  • Higher = taller plants faster
  • Multiplied by vigor, moisture, etiolation
growthIncrementWidth 0.0001

Base amount plants grow wider per cycle (before modifiers).

Impact:
  • Higher = thicker plants faster
  • Higher = root radius grows faster
  • Affects competition priority
‚ö†Ô∏è
Critical parameter: Width growth is 20x slower than length by default. Even small changes dramatically affect competition balance.
rootDistanceMultiplier 1.0

How much width growth expands root radius.

Impact:
  • Formula: rootRadius = 1.0 + (this √ó widthGrowth)
  • Higher = wider plants dominate more
  • Lower = width matters less

üíß Moisture Parameters

minMoisture 0.3

Minimum moisture level plants can use (0-1 range).

Impact:
  • Higher = fewer usable tiles (harder survival)
  • Lower = more tiles available (easier)
maxMoisture 0.7

Maximum moisture level plants can use (0-1 range).

Impact:
  • Lower = fewer usable tiles (harder survival)
  • Higher = more tiles available (easier)
optimalMoisture 0.5

Sweet spot for fastest growth (between min and max).

Impact:
  • Distance from this = growth penalty
  • At edges (min/max) = 50% growth rate
marginalMoistureGrowthRate 0.5

Growth rate multiplier at min/max moisture edges.

Impact:
  • 1.0 = no penalty at edges
  • 0.5 = 50% growth at edges (default)
  • 0.0 = can only grow at exact optimal
moistureDecrement 0.01

How much moisture plants extract per growth cycle.

Impact:
  • Higher = soil dries faster
  • Higher = more rain needed
  • Balance with rain's moistureIncrement

‚öîÔ∏è Competition Parameters

etiolationStrength 2.0

How extreme the tall/thin vs short/wide growth becomes.

Impact:
  • 2.0 = crowded plants 2x taller, 0.5x wider
  • 1.0 = no etiolation effect
  • 3.0 = extreme tall/thin (3x, 0.33x)
‚ö†Ô∏è
High values (>2.5): Crowded plants become too thin, lose all water competition, mass death events.
competitionRadius 2.0

(Currently unused in code - for future features)

Impact:
  • No effect in current version
  • Placeholder for future competition mechanics

‚ò†Ô∏è Death Parameters

timeToDie 10s

How long (seconds) a plant can survive without water before wilting.

Impact:
  • Higher = more forgiving (plants survive droughts)
  • Lower = harsher (quick death from competition loss)
  • Once wilting starts, death is inevitable
‚ö†Ô∏è
Too low (<5s): Mass die-offs from temporary water shortages. Too high (>20s): Weak plants linger too long, overpopulation.

‚òî Rain Parameters (ManagerRain)

raindropCount 200

How many raindrops fall simultaneously across the world.

Impact:
  • Higher = more frequent moisture replenishment
  • Balance with moistureDecrement
moistureIncrement (Soil) 0.1

How much moisture each raindrop adds to a tile.

Impact:
  • Higher = faster soil recharge
  • Must balance with plant extraction rate
speed 8.0

How fast raindrops fall (visual only).

Impact:
  • Purely visual - doesn't affect moisture rate
  • Affected by Time Scale
